statement

My method of work is based on the exploration of the dual nature of reality. Contradictions may be perhaps the only way to explain a little bit about life, as well as one of the fullest expressions of lucidity.

Through my work I seek to put opposite elements in dialogue: the real and the imagined, the artificial and the natural, reason and absurdity, the abstract and the tangible. Through the image I propose a reformulation of these polarities with the intention of dissolving them and reaching a point of integration.
